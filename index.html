About Me



Hi, my name is Yusuf Effendi. My friends usually call me "Yusuf" or "Fendi". I am a self-taught Freelance Graphic Designer based in Jombang-Indonesia with experience working with some of the greatest artists and bands in the music industry. My Design is focused on Lettering art and logotype for any type of business such as a Band, Clothing, Label etc. Even though my design is broadly identical to Metal music, I've also made some that are simpler and less-metal. With some of the things I have done, I have the opportunity to expand my design skills into other fields and styles.

These are some of the names I've worked with :

  • Disentomb
  • Analepsy
  • Revenge The Fate
  • Bosedeath
  • And many more...
